Medal criteria summary

Gold
Integrated Human Practices
Our Integrated Human Practices is focused on closing the loop on the use of PET. Beyond the criteria of the silver, we further visited a number of enterprises related to the use and production of PET, including the famous COFCO Coca-Cola Beverages. What’s more, advice from foreign professors proficient in protein engineering really helps us to design the more suitable enzyme for industrial. We also visited the local government and discussed the situation and solutions of plastic pollution which helped us further develop our plans and make our products closer to reality. To see more, please go to Human_Practices Page.
